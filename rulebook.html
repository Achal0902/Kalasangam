  1. A maximum of two (02) teams from each college may participate in this competition.
  2. Each team must consist of two (2) participants.

  4. The first speaker will speak in favor of the topic, and the second speaker will speak against the topic.
  5. Participants may present in Marathi, Hindi, or English. Both participants may use the same or different languages; however, each participant must complete their presentation in a single language.
  6. Each participant will be given a minimum of four (4) minutes and a maximum of five (5) minutes. The time will begin immediately after the announcement of the participant's name.
  7. Personal criticism, abusive language, disrespectful remarks, and interruption while the opponent is speaking are strictly prohibited.
  8. Reading from written material during the presentation is not allowed.
  9. If any participant uses offensive words regarding religion, caste, language, or gender, the participant will be disqualified.

  11. The decision of the judges (panel) shall be final and binding.
